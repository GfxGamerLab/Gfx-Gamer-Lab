📢 ADSTERRA ADS MANAGEMENT

Add your Adsterra ad codes below. Toggle each ad ON/OFF to control visibility on your website.

🔗 SMARTLINK (First Download Click)

⭐ Highest Earning! Opens when user clicks download first time. Second click starts download.

1️⃣ TOP BANNER AD

Shows at top of page. Use 728x90 for desktop, 320x50 for mobile.

2️⃣ BELOW HERO AD

Shows between hero section and posts. Native banner works best here.

3️⃣ IN-CONTENT AD

Shows in middle of posts grid. Blends well with content.

4️⃣ PRE-DOWNLOAD AD (Countdown Screen)

⭐ High Conversion! Shows during 5-second countdown before download.

5️⃣ FOOTER AD

Shows at bottom of page. Social bar ad format works well.

💡 Adsterra Setup Guide

  1. Go to Adsterra Dashboard
  2. Create ads: Banner (728x90), Native Banner, Social Bar, Smartlink
  3. Copy ad code from each ad
  4. Paste codes in respective fields above
  5. Toggle ON the ads you want to show
  6. Save and check your website!

Pro Tip: Enable Smartlink + Pre-Download for maximum earnings!
